# Tata-Motors-Demerger-Strategic-Change-Management
Strategic Analysis | Real Case Study

MBA assignment analysing the demerger of Tata Motors into two business units (CV & PV). Focused on evaluating change management strategies and designing a structured implementation plan.

🎯 Key Areas
Change Management Models- Kotter's 
Organisational Culture & Change
Role of Change Agents
Employee Engagement
Internal & External Drivers of Change

🔍 Key Insights
Demerger improves strategic focus and efficiency
Employee engagement is critical for successful change
Culture and leadership strongly influence outcomes

💡 Recommendations
Strong communication strategy
Use of change agents to manage resistance
Focus on employee training & engagement
